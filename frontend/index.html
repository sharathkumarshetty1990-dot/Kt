What You Can Actually Say

Real commands. Real results.

These are actual natural language inputs Linguist understands and executes. None of them exist as buttons anywhere else.

graphic_eq

"Shake the frame violently on every beat, white flash on the drop"

Beat detection to amplitude-50 shake with xy axis to flash overlay triggered at peak beats. Zero manual keyframes.

water

"Make it look like the footage was shot underwater"

Blue-green colorbalance + wave distortion + gaussian blur + low-pass audio filter. Reasoned from scratch.

movie_filter

"Lofi aesthetic. Warm tones, vinyl crackle feel, faded film"

Warm colorbalance + reduced saturation + film grain + soft blur + vignette + high-frequency audio rolloff.

bolt

"Make it feel aggressive. High contrast, desaturated, heavy bass"

eq contrast 1.6 + hue saturation 0.4 + hard vignette + glitch on beats + 10dB bass boost at 60Hz.

auto_awesome

"Cinematic. Teal shadows, orange highlights, letterbox"

colorbalance teal/orange grade + crop to 2.39:1 + subtle grain + vignette. Classic blockbuster look.

psychology

"Make it feel like a memory. Fading, warm, dreamlike, slow"

Warm grade + gblur glow + setpts slow motion + aecho reverb + slight overexposure. No presets. Pure reasoning.
